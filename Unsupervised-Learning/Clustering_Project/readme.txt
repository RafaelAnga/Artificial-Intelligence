#### Market Segmentation Analysis

- **Description**: Customer segmentation analysis using K-means clustering to group customers based on their Annual Income and Spending Score
- **Technologies**: Python, scikit-learn, numpy, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn, yellowbrick
- **Algorithms**: K-means Clustering
- **Key Findings**: 
  - Identified 5 distinct customer segments
  - Group 1: Low income (20K-40K), low spending (0-40)
  - Group 2: Low income (20K-40K), high spending (60-100)
  - Group 3: Average income (40K-75K), average spending (40-60)
  - Group 4: High income (75K-140K), low spending (0-40)
  - Group 5: High income (75K-140K), high spending (60-100)
- **Visualization**: Includes elbow method plot for optimal cluster selection and scatter plot showing customer segments
